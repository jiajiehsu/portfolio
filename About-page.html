Hi! I’m jay, a freelancer in architectural design from Taiwan.

I’ve been designing for over 3 years and I’ve gained experience working alongside visionaries in architectural studio and engineering consultants company. Most of my clients are architectural, interior and engineering firms, and I assist in bring their vision to life from mere sketches on a napkin to living space.

建築設計

在我的經驗裡，經手過的項目從天花板至帷幕牆因不同材料及其功能而有不同的表現及系統，而我針對這些要求而創造出解決辦法。

Architectural Design

In my experience, I have worked on projects from ceiling to curtain wall that have different performance and system depending on the material and its function. I have to find solutions to address these requirements.

設計與運算

結合著參數控制與程式撰寫，以此作為創作媒材，突破過往技術限制，讓許多案例得以實踐，好比建築幾何與合理化分割，用這樣的方法將項目設計並製造。

Computational Design

A combination of parametric control and programming is used as a creative medium to break through the technical limitations in the past, allowing many projects to be implemented, such as architectural geometry and rationalization of segmentation, designing and manufacturing in this way.

資訊模型(BIM)

用數位化的建築元件表示真實世界中用來建造建築物的構件。並以此查詢各類構建在空間中彼此衝突或干涉情形的詳細資訊。通過視覺化程式的撰寫來提取之中的數據。

BIM

A digital representation of the building components used to construct buildings in the real world. This is used to find out more about the various types of conflict or interference situations in whole life circle of building. All of building data is extracted through the visualization of the program.
